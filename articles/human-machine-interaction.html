Human–Machine Interaction

The study of how humans communicate, collaborate, and coexist with intelligent systems.

Interface Evolution

Interaction has progressed from command lines to graphical interfaces to voice, gesture, and neural inputs.

Cognitive Coupling

Modern systems increasingly act as cognitive partners, influencing attention, decision-making, and learning.

Augmentation vs Replacement

Human–machine systems may amplify human capability rather than replace it, reshaping productivity and creativity.

Ethical Design

Design choices affect autonomy, trust, and dependency, requiring careful governance.
